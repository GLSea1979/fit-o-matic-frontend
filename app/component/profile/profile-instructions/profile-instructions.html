Welcome to your profile page. Here you can input your measurements and find bikes that feel built for you. You can save your favorite bikes, upload a picture of yourself and save your profile information.

You will need to enter your height and inseam length. You may use either inches or centimeters but the measurement needs to be a whole number.

    In order to acurately measure your inseam:
  • 1. Stand up against a wall with your feet 6-8 inches apart.
  • 2. Push a book, with the spine facing up, into your crotch with similar pressure to when you are sitting on a bike seat.
  • 3. Hold the book against the wall while you move and then measure the distance from the floor to the top of the book.
